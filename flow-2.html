BUILD YOUR PORTFOLIO

Produce an online version and document your code and project structure on Github. What’s expected:

  • You upload your portfolio onto your webhotel
  • Create a repository for your portfolio project
  • Create a decent project structure (file names and folders)
  • Error free code

    • Besides the technical implements, it is important you consider:

  • How do I present myself in the best possible way?
  • How do I communicate what I’ve learned so far and hope to learn in the future?
  • Which visual style and mood do I wish my portolio to portray?
  • How do I give my portfolio a personal touch?
