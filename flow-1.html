PHOTO EDITING & PORTFOLIO

Pick atleast 1 photo from the excursion. It is not required that the photo was one you took yourself, but no stock pictures.

The photo(s) needs to be treated in Photoshop under these requirements:

  • Remove an element: An element in the photo has to be removed and the background needs to blend with the photo.
  • Add an element: Add something that wasnt originally in the photo. It needs to look natural like nothing was added to it.
  • Edit and adjust the lighting and give the photo character with filters or color tones.
  • Crop and align the photo.
  • The format of the image needs to be 4:3 and needs to be saved as < 1 M pixel
  • Make sure to upload the orginial image in the same format as the edited one.
